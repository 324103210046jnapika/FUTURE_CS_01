# Task 2 – Phishing Email Detection

## Objective
To identify phishing emails and understand common phishing techniques used by attackers.

## What is Phishing?
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords or bank details.

## Example Phishing Email

Subject: Urgent! Verify your bank account

Dear User,

Your bank account will be suspended within 24 hours. Please verify your account using the link below.

http://fake-bank-login.com

## Indicators of Phishing
- Suspicious links
- Urgent messages
- Unknown sender
- Spelling mistakes
- Requests for personal information

## Prevention Tips
- Do not click suspicious links
- Verify sender email address
- Use email security filters
- Enable multi-factor authentication

## Conclusion
Phishing attacks are common social engineering attacks. Awareness and careful email verification can help prevent such attacks.
